Q2. Define Suppurative Inflammation 
Answer: 
Suppurative inflammation is an acute inflammation characterized by pus formation. 
It may be localized (abscess) or diffuse (cellulitis). 
 
Q3. Define Abscess with cause and mechanism 
Answer: 
An abscess is a localized acute suppurative inflammation forming a cavity filled with 
pus. 
• Causative organism: Staphylococcus 
• Mechanism: Production of coagulase enzyme → excess fibrin → localization of 
infection 
 
Q4. Define Boil (Furuncle) 
Answer: 
A boil is a small abscess arising from a hair follicle or sebaceous gland.
