Management 
• Surgical :
To remove necrotic bone or drain abscess
• Medical : 
    Antibiotics
   Pain management
   Immobilization 
   Hyperbaric oxygen therapy

Hyperbaric oxygen therapy : closed space with 
oxygen higher than in normal air pressure. It provide 
the body tissues with pure oxygen

It may be individualized for one patient , or group 
therapy

Nursing process  
1- Assessment :
• Monitor for fever, pain, swelling, and 
wound drainage
• Check lab results (WBC, CRP)
• Assess for risk factors (diabetes, recent 
surgeries)
2- Nursing diagnosis :
• Acute pain related to infection
• Impaired physical mobility related to pain
• Risk for sepsis
• Risk for impaired skin integrity

Nursing process  
3- planning  :
• Manage pain
• Reduce infection
• Prevent complications
• Promote mobility
4- implementation :
• Administer antibiotics as prescribed
• Wound care
• Educate on medication adherence and 
signs of infection
• Promote rest and immobilization

Nursing process  
5- Evaluation :
• Reduction in signs of 
infection
• Decreased pain
• Improved mobility
• No complications ( as sepsis)

Osteoporosis
Definition
      Decrease bone mass and deterioration of bone tissue, leading 
to fragile bones and increased risk of fractures.

Aging
 (postmenopausal 
women)
Hormonal changes 
(↓ estrogen or 
testosterone)
Long-term use of 
corticosteroids
Malnutrition 
(low calcium, 
vitamin D)
Sedentary lifestyle
Causes

• Assess for risk factors (age, diet, lifestyle)
• Review bone mineral density (BMD) results
• Monitor for signs of fractures
