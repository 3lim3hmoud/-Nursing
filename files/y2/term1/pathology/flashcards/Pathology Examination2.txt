Part II: Essay & Short Answer Questions

Q1. Discuss the Etiology of Granuloma 
Answer: 
The causes of granuloma are classified into: 
1. Infectious Causes: 
• Bacterial: Tuberculosis, Leprosy 
• Parasitic: Bilharziasis 
• Fungal: Histoplasmosis 
2. Non-Infectious Causes: 
• Foreign body: Silicosis, surgical sutures 
• Autoimmune diseases: Sarcoidosis, Crohn’s disease
