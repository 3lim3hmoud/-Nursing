Angina Pectoris

ذبحة الصدر — ملخص المحاضرة
Definition & Pathophysiology
التعريف والفيزيولوجيا المرضية
  • Angina pectoris = clinical syndrome caused by myocardial ischemia when O₂ demand exceeds supply during exertion
  • Felt as: chest discomfort / tightness ± radiation to left arm, shoulder, or jaw
  • Main cause: blockage of coronary arteries (coronary artery disease) by atherosclerotic plaque
  • Ischemia types: Demand ischemia (fixed stenosis) Supply ischemia (spasm / thrombus)
Key Numbers
أرقام مهمة
  • SaO₂ target: > 90%
  • O₂ dose: 2–4 L/min nasal cannula
  • NTG sublingual: 0.3–0.4 mg × 3 q5 min
  • NTG IV: 10 µg/min titrated
  • Morphine IV: 2–4 mg push
  • Fibrinolysis: within 30 min (STEMI)
  • PCI: within 90 min (STEMI)
Types of Angina
أنواع الذبحة الصدرية
Stable Angina
ذبحة مستقرة
Pain only during exertion or extreme emotional stress. Disappears with rest. Fixed stenosis → demand ischemia.
Demand Ischemia
Unstable Angina (UA)
ذبحة غير مستقرة
Pain at rest or minimal exertion, increasing frequency/severity. Partial/intermittent thrombus. Biomarkers NOT elevated.
Supply Ischemia
Prinzmetal / Variant
ذبحة برنزمتال
Occurs at rest, during sleep, or cold exposure. Caused by transient coronary artery spasm, not plaque.
Vasospasm

Atherosclerosis Progression — مراحل تصلب الشرايين
🔴
Normal Artery
شريان طبيعي
Smooth lumen, unobstructed blood flow
🟡
Early Stage
المرحلة المبكرة
Cholesterol entry → inflammation → plaque formation
🟠
Intermediate
المرحلة المتوسطة
Plaque build-up, arterial wall bulging, ↑ inflammation
🔶
Late Stage
المرحلة المتأخرة
Vulnerable plaque, limited blood flow, prone to rupture
💥
Plaque Rupture
انفجار اللويحة
Clot → heart attack or stroke
