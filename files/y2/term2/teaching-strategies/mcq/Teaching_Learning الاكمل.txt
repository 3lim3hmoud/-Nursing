Teaching is a __________ process of facilitating learning that 
involves planning, delivering, and evaluating instruction. 
Answer: deliberate

Learning is a lifelong process of transforming information and 
experience into __________, __________, and __________. 
Answer: knowledge, skills, and attitudes 
  Q3   
Education is a __________ process involving the interplay of 
educator, educand, and social forces. 
Answer: dynamic 
  Q4   
The four components of the education process are: Learning 
Environment, Teacher, __________, and Learner. 
Answer: Curriculum 
  Q5   
Teaching as a social process has three focal points: Teacher, 
Student, and __________. 
Answer: Subject Matter 
  Q6   
An __________ learning atmosphere helps everyone to grow 
simultaneously. 
Answer: inclusive 
  Q7   
The teacher is the most important factor in assuring the __________ 
of education. 
Answer: quality 
  Q8   
Learning produces a __________ change in thinking, feeling, and 
doing. 
Answer: permanent 
  Q9   
Creativity can be developed and innovation benefits both 
__________ and __________. 
Answer: students and teachers 
  Q10   
The curriculum is a series of things a student must __________ and 
__________ to develop learning abilities. 
Answer: perform and experience

Part D: Bloom's Taxonomy (Q29–Q35) 
  Q29   
Bloom's original Taxonomy of Educational Objectives was published 
in __________. 
Answer: 1956 
  Q30   
The 2001 revision of Bloom's Taxonomy was conducted by 
__________ and __________. 
Answer: Anderson and Krathwohl 
  Q31   
In the revised 2001 taxonomy, the six levels from lowest to highest 
end with: ...Analyze, __________, and __________. 
Answer: Evaluate, Create 
  Q32   
The three domains of Bloom's framework are the Cognitive domain 
(________), the Affective domain (________), and the Psychomotor 
domain (________). 
Answer: Thinking / Feeling / Doing 
  Q33   
In the ABCD model, A = Audience, B = __________, C = Condition, 
D = __________. 
Answer: Behavior / Degree 
  Q34   
A learning Goal is __________, long-term, and NOT directly 
measurable, while a learning Objective is __________, observable, 
and measurable. 
Answer: broad / specific 
  Q35   
The verb '________' signals the Create (L6) cognitive level, while 
the verb '________' signals the Remember (L1) level. 
Answer: Design / Define
