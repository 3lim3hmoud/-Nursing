(Intermediate locally aggressive tumors)Characterized by:1.Slower rate of growth than frank malignant tumors.2.Criteria of anaplasia as the malignant tumors but with lower grade. 3.Locally invasive and destructive to the surrounding tissues.4.No distant metastasis.

Prognosis: Much better than malignant tumors. 1- Local recurrence is common after surgical excision.2- They might rarely change into frank malignant tumors that metastasize.•Examples of locally malignant tumors:1.Basal cell carcinoma.2.Giant cell tumor of bone (osteoctastoma).
