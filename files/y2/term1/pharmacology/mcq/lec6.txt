Part II: Essay Model Hints 
1. Right side: Deoxygenated blood to lungs (Pulmonary circulation). Left side: 
Oxygenated blood to tissues (Systemic circulation).

2. Lub (1st): Ventricles contract, AV valves (Mitral/Tricuspid) close. Dub (2nd): 
Ventricles relax, Semilunar valves (Aortic/Pulmonary) close. 
3. Automaticity (Generate stimulus), Rhythmicity (Regular beating), Contractility 
(Push blood), Excitability (Respond to stimulus), Conductivity (Conduct stimulus). 
4. Preload: Load at end of diastole; depends on venous return. Afterload: 
Resistance to open aortic valve; depends on peripheral resistance. 
5. Pathway: SA node → Atria contract → AV node → AV Bundle (Bundle of His) → 
Bundle branches → Purkinje fibers → Ventricles contract.
